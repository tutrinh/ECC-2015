What is the Instructor Search?

The Instructor Search allows Instructors who are interested in training opportunities in addition to the training they provide for their current Training Center to be "searchable" to Training Center Coordinators and AHA National Center. As part of the Instructor Search, you can indicate the courses you are willing to teach, and the states and languages in which you can provide courses.
